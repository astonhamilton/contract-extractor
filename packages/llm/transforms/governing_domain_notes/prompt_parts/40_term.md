Term domain:
- Questions answered:
  - When does the agreement take effect and how long does it run?
  - How does renewal or extension work?
- Explain the effective-term structure in plain language.
- Distinguish execution/effective language from operational timing when the document does so.
- Keep operative term separate from project schedule, notice-to-proceed timing, anticipated work windows, testing windows, installation timing, or other operational milestones unless the document clearly makes those the contract term.
- Do not force a precise normalized date role when the document only gives a nuanced or project-completion-based term structure.
- If renewal or extension is optional or nuanced, explain it rather than over-normalizing it.
